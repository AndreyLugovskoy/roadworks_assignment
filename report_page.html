General information

 

Datafiles contain information about roadworks, that happened in 101 regions in Great Britain in the period between 2011-01-17 and 2018-04-30. Roadworks were performed on 132 roads. The longest road work lasts between 2011-01-17 and 2016-12-31 and takes 2174 days 09:46:00 h:m:s.The shortest road work lasts between 2016-03-14 and 2016-03-14 and takes 0 days 01:00:00 h:m:s.

 

The distribution of works on the territory of UK is presented on the map. As seen, only data on England was included in the datasets, as data on Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland is missing.

 

The simple analysis, based on number of authorities, responsible for works and area value of this municipalities shown, that the longest road in dataset and, probably in UK is A1, which turned out to true in both cases.

 

 

 

Delays and works during Easter holidays 2016

 

        

  Bank holidays in UK in 2016 lasted for four days, starting from 2016-03-25. According to our data there are 693 works between 2016-03-25 and 2016-03-28 in 82 regions of UK.  Most of the works (61) will take place on the roads of the County of Surrey. It can be seen, that the road west to London is pretty busy with a lot of works, that is where Surrey is located.
